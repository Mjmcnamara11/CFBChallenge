League Rules
Official
01
Snake Draft
All managers participate in a snake draft, each selecting 20 college football teams (100 picks total). Draft order reverses each round (1-2-3-4-5, 5-4-3-2-1…). Your 20 teams are locked for the entire season.
02
Weekly Scoring
Each week, every win recorded by one of your 20 teams scores a point. The manager whose teams record the most combined wins that week earns 1 win in the season standings.
03
Tiebreaker
Ties broken by total point differential across all your teams' games that week. If still tied, the week is split and both managers receive 0.5 wins.
04
Season Champion
Most weekly wins after the regular season wins the title and earns first pick in next year's draft.
05
Bowl Bonus
Bowl wins = 1 pt. CFP Semifinal wins = 2 pts. National Championship win = 3 pts. Bowl champion earns eternal bragging rights.
